Dice Game

Game Rules:

1. Roll the dice

2. 1's are worth 100 points

3. 5's are worth 50 points

4. If you roll a 1 or 5, you can choose to keep your points and walk away, or risk them all to roll again.

5. If you roll a hand without a 1 or 5, you lose all your points and lose the game.

6. See if you can reach 1,000 points!
